# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 7, 2026**

Welcome to Unix Timestamp Converter (timestamp-tool.com). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information related to your use of this tool.

## 1. Information We Collect

This tool is a **client-side only** web application. All timestamp conversions are performed entirely in your browser. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any input data you enter into the converter.

We may collect limited, anonymous usage data through third-party analytics services (e.g., Vercel Analytics) to help us understand how the tool is used and improve the user experience. This data may include:

- Pages visited
- General geographic region (country/city level)
- Browser type and device type
- Referral source

This data is **aggregated and anonymized** — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

---

## 2. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or store any personal identifiers in your browser. Any cookies set by third-party analytics providers are used solely for aggregate usage measurement.

## 3. Third-Party Services

## 4. Data Security
